Kohna Meros

Kohna Meros is a project to provide an immersive 3D visualization of ancient architecture
in culturally significant city of Bukhara.

Its collection presents over 30 monuments, allowing comfortable planning for tourists, as well as safe preservation in a digital format.

“Heritage is our legacy from the past, what we live with today, and what we pass on to future
generations. Our cultural and natural heritage are both irreplaceable sources of life and
inspiration.” - UNESCO (World Heritage)
